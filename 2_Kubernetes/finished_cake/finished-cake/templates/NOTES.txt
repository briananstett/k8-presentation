Thank you for installing {{ .Chart.Name }}.

Your release is named {{ .Release.Name }}.

To learn more about the release, try:

  $ helm status {{ .Release.Name }}
  $ helm get {{ .Release.Name }}

To get the service account's ca.crt and token run:
  $ kubectl get secrets --namespace {{.Release.Namespace}}

Then find the secret for your service account and run:
  $ kuebctl get secret <service-account's secret> -o yaml

To get the service's nodePort run:
  $ echo $(kube get service {{ .Release.Name }}-service -o jsonpath="{.spec.ports[0].nodePort}")\n
